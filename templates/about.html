About CropGuard

CropGuard is an AI-powered disease detection system specifically designed for tomato plants. Using cutting-edge technology, it helps farmers diagnose and manage plant diseases efficiently, reducing reliance on pesticides and improving crop yield quality.

CropGuard uses a simple, user-friendly interface where farmers can upload an image of a tomato plant leaf and get disease predictions along with recommended treatments.
